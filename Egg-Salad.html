Homemade Egg Salad

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mustard
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Cooking Directions

  1. Bring water in a pot to a boil
  2. Add eggs to boiling water
  3. Let eggs boil for 10 minutes
  4. Remove eggs from water and put them in a bowl of cold water
  5. Remove shells from eggs and place in a bowl
  6. Mash eggs with a fork
  7. Add 1 tbsp of mustard and 2 tbsp of Mayonaise
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Enjoy!
