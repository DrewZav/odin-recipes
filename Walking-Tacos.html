Walking Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • mild taco seasoning
  • bell peppers
  • doritos
  • sourcream
  • Hot sauce of your choice
  • cheese

Cooking Directions

  1. Cook Hamburger, drain grease and mix taco seasoning
  2. Dice bell peppers and cook until liking
  3. Smash doritos onto a plate
  4. add seasoned hamburger, sour cream, hot sauce and cheese
  5. Enjoy!
