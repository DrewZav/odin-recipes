Aldi Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice, I personally choose rigatoni

  • 5 Cheese Sauce from Aldi

  • Parmasean Cheese

    • Cooking Directions

    1. Boil water in a pot
    2. Boil pasta noodles for 10-12 minutes
    3. Strain pasta and add sauce to the pot that used to have water
    4. Add pasta to sauce and mix(add Parmasean cheese if wanted)
    5. Enjoy!
