Merry Me Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Garlic
  • Sundried Tomatoes
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • flour

Cooking Directions

  1. Cover Chicken in seasoning then Flour and seasoning
  2. Cook Chicken in pan with butter until crust forms and chicken is done
  3. Cook sauce using dried tomatoes x amount of heavy whipping cream, x amount of chicken broth and x amount of Parmesean cheese
  4. Once sauce becomes thick cook pasta noodles
  5. When noodles are done mix all together with sauce
  6. cut chicken into pieces and add chicken into sauce
  7. Stir all together and load your plate!
