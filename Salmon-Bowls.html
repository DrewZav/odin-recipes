Salmon Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Salmon with or without the skin
  • Seaweed wrap
  • spicy mayonnaisse
  • White rice

Cooking Directions

  1. Season fish with preferred seasoning
  2. Cook salmon and let sit
  3. Cook rice of choice
  4. Mix rice and fish together
  5. Add seaweed wrap but crush up and add spicy mayo Enjoy!
